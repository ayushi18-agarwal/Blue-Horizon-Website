Join the global River Cleanup community

There are many ways to become part of our mission for clean rivers. You can clean a river yourself, support our activities or reduce your own plastic use. Any way you want to take action is fantastic. We are very grateful for every single person contributing to our common goal to make this world a better place!

Some monumental moments:



Cleanups

Cleanups are a great way to take your first steps towards clean rivers! People come together to collect waste that has ended up along or in rivers. You can meet like-minded locals and have a direct positive impact on a river near you. Check out the upcoming events or organize your own cleanup!


Reduce your plastic

By using less single-use plastic in your life, you can reduce the amount of plastic that ends up in nature and rivers. We made a database to help you on your way to reduce your plastic use! Check out the practical tips and alternatives to lower your plastic consumption!
