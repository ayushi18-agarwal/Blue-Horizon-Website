River Cleanup Event 2024

Event Details

On June 15, 2024, volunteers from across the city came together for a massive cleanup of the River Yamuna. Organized by Green Earth Initiative, the event focused on removing waste, raising awareness, and restoring the river's natural beauty.

The cleanup spanned over 10 kilometers of riverbank and lasted 8 hours. Volunteers, including school students, environmentalists, and local residents, joined hands to make this event a success.

Our Achievements

During the event, we accomplished:

  • Collected over 5 tons of waste, including plastic, metal, and other pollutants.
  • Planted 500 trees along the riverbank to prevent soil erosion.
  • Engaged 1,000+ volunteers, fostering a sense of community and environmental responsibility.
