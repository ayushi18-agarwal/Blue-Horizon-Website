Welcome to Blue horizon Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to restoring and protecting our rivers for future generations. Founded with a vision to combat pollution and preserve natural waterways, we work tirelessly to ensure our rivers flow clean and vibrant, supporting ecosystems and communities alike.




Our Mission

Our mission is to rejuvenate rivers by removing pollutants, promoting sustainable practices, and raising awareness about the critical role of clean water in our lives. We aim to empower individuals, communities, and industries to take collective action towards a cleaner, healthier planet.




What We Do?

  • River Cleanup Drives: Organizing large-scale and community-driven cleanups to remove waste from rivers and their banks.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Educating people on the importance of keeping rivers clean through workshops, events, and social media initiatives.
  • Partnerships: Collaborating with local authorities, businesses, and volunteers to implement long-term solutions for waterway conservation.
  • Research and Advocacy: Conducting studies on pollution levels and advocating for policies that protect and rejuvenate our rivers.



Why It Matters?

Rivers are the lifelines of our ecosystems, providing water, food, and livelihoods to millions. However, pollution, overuse, and neglect have pushed many of these vital resources to the brink of collapse. By addressing these issues, we are not just cleaning rivers—we are creating a sustainable future for all.
